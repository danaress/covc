About colo.vc

I started colo.vc to answer two simple questions:

1. What companies in Colorado are raising money?

2. Where are Colorado-based VCs investing their money?

The first iteration of colo.vc was a more traditional blog. After a couple of months of typing out blog posts manually, I wanted to start simplifying the experience for both myself and readers.

The next phases of development are:

Automating the collection of funding information and adding a section for VC activity.

Automated notifications for new data.

Reporting and visualizations based on the data.
